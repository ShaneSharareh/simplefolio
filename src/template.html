Pig Game

Pig is a dice game where you must roll a 2 through 6, each number being accumulated for the current the player's score. If your score reaches to 100 before the other player, you win the game. However, if your die lands on a 0, you lose all of your points

You are given an option to hold your turn and score and pass the die to the other player until they either pass it back or lose.
