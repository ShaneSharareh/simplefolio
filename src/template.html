About me

Hello, my name is Shane. I’m passionate about programming and app development because people rely so heavily on technology, and a well-designed program can make a significantly positive impact on people’s lives. Whether the app is for productivity or entertainment.

I graduated in 2017 with a B.S. in Computer Science and have also completed a web & mobile apps development program held at SDSU for continuing education. For several years, I actively provided technical support at my church to enable the delivery of seamless experiences for the congregation.

Other than programming, I have a passion for music and fitness, my hobbies include: playing the guitar, going on hikes and working out regularly at my gym
